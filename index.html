About

Hello, I'm Jen. I design and build custom websites, and maintain existing web and e-commerce sites hosted on platforms such as Wix and WordPress. I founded Purple 41 Web Design in 2023 after branching out as a freelance web developer when a family member asked me to enhance their existing business website hosted on Wix. Take a look at the Projects section for examples of some of my recent work.

Do you need a website and have an idea in mind but not sure where to begin? Need to restructure an existing website to make it more user-friendly or add new features? Or perhaps you're rebranding and want to build a brand new site? I can help, from one-off or short-term projects to ongoing website maintenance.

Services

Custom website design tailored to your brand and requirements

Adding new features to your existing website with optional ongoing site maintenance

Restructuring your website to make it more user-friendly and accessible

Rebuilding your website from the ground up, for a relaunch or rebrand
